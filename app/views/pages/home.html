Chalk Theory Bandits is a band that was formed in October 2012 by David Stahl, Alex Yau, Sebastien Issa, Jason Littman, Paul Calhoun, and Jordyn Tumas—all current students at the University of Pennsylvania. It is one of the few cover bands on Penn's 20,000 person campus and is best known for its unique combination of instruments, most notably its addition of an extra guitar, an extra piano and the lack of a bass.

Chalk Theory Bandits covers a variety of genres, yet mostly focuses on trending college music for live performances. The band is best known for its cover of “Too Close” by Alex Clare, which dramatically showcases the talents of “the bandits.” These include Tumas’s stirring vocals, Yau and Stahl’s synth capabilities on piano, Calhoun’s vast array of sounds on percussion, and Littman and Issa’s creatively composed guitar tracks. The translation of “Too Close’s” dubstep chorus into a vibrant live set is a exhilarating performance you don't want to miss.
